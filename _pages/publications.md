## Job Market Paper

### A Dynamic Model of Residential Sorting Using Aggregate Data: Urban Fracking and Environmental Justice in North Texas

[Draft](/files/jmp-draft.pdf){: .btn .btn--primary}
[Slides](/files/jmp-slides.pdf){: .btn}
[Poster](/files/jmp-poster.pdf){: .btn}

**Abstract.**  
This paper studies how urban fracking affects residential sorting, housing markets, and welfare. I develop a dynamic residential sorting model using aggregate data to quantify how environmental disamenities shape neighborhood choice and distributional outcomes across households.
